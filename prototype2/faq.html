Frequently asked questions




1. What time is check-in and check-out?

  • Check-in: From 3.00 PM
  • Check-out: By 11.00 AM

Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request (subject to availability and extra charges).


2. Do you offer aiport shuttle services?

Yes, we offer airport transfers. Please contact our front desk at least 24 hours in advance to arrange pickup
(charges may apply).


3. Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer free on-site parking for all our guests. Valet service is also available.


4. Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast options vary based on your booking. Some room rates include breakfast-please check your
reservation details or ask at the front desk.


5. Are pets allowed?

Pets are not allowed, except for certified service animals.

6. Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel.

7. What amenities does the hotel offer?

Our amenities include:

  • Fitness Center
  • Outdoor Pool
  • Business center
  • On-Site restaurant & Bar
  • Room service
  • 24-hour front desk

8. Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Cancellation Policies vary depending on your booking. Please refer to your reservation confirmation or
contact us directly for details.


9. Do you have a accessible rooms?

Yes, we offer accessible rooms and facilities for guests with disabilities. Please inform us of any special
reqirements when booking.

10. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and cash. A valid ID and credit card are required at
check-in.
